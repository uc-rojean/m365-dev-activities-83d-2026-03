# Governance & Accountability

This document defines how development activities,
documentation, and external dependencies are governed.

Its purpose is to maintain consistency, accountability,
and traceability across long-running work.

---


## Decision Ownership

- Development decisions are documented and reviewed daily through UC logs
- External dependency decisions (e.g., Microsoft Support) are tracked separately
- No undocumented changes are introduced during incident periods

## Scope Discipline

- One issue per support case, in line with Microsoft support policy
- SP/OD-dependent tasks are deferred until access is restored
- All workaround activity is documented and reversible

---


## Continuity Rules

- Development continues even during service outages
- Documentation-only days are valid and logged honestly
- All delays and skips are explicitly recorded, not hidden
