# Risk & Mitigation Log

This document tracks known risks that may impact
development continuity, delivery, or system stability.

Each risk includes a mitigation strategy to ensure
progress continues even under constraints.

---


## Active Risks

### 1. SharePoint / OneDrive Access Denied
- **Status:** Ongoing since October 2025
- **Impact:** Blocks SP/OD-dependent development and access to stored data
- **Mitigation:**
  - Continue SP/OD-independent development
  - Maintain GitHub-based documentation and planning
  - Actively engage Microsoft Support until access is restored

### 2. External Dependency Delays (Support Response Time)
- **Status:** Intermittent
- **Impact:** Slows resolution of tenant-level issues
- **Mitigation:**
  - Document all interactions and timelines
  - Prepare execution-ready artifacts in advance
  - Escalate appropriately when required

---


### 3. Post-Recovery Catch-Up Pressure
- **Status:** Potential
- **Impact:** Risk of rushing tasks once SP/OD access is restored
- **Mitigation:**
  - Follow ROADMAP.md phases
  - Use readiness checklists
  - Prioritize stability over speed
